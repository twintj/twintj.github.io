Tom Williams

INTJ LLC

Tom Williams is a scriptwriter, producer and director specializing in health care communications. For more than twenty-five years, he has been creating highly effective, high-end video programs for major medical providers, including:

  • Patient education for cardiology, endocrinology, oncology, gastroenterology, urology, psychiatry, orthopedics and other specialties
  • Recruiting for residents, nurses and medical students
  • Award-winning documentaries, fictional films and plays on remarkable people and events in the history of medicine

His work has been recognized with Gold Medals from the New York Festivals, Golden Reels from the International Television Association, Gold Awards from the National Health Information Awards and Accolade Awards from the Accolade Global Film Competition.

Prior to this work, Tom Williams was a Senior Vice President at Campbell Mithun Esty Advertising and Creative Director of CME Video & Film, the agency's non-broadcast production facility. In 1996, he started INTJ to pursue his passion for creating effective, encouraging, engaging programs that help physicians and nurses teach their patients how to improve their health and get the best outcomes from their health care.
